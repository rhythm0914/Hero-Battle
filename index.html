How is this calculated?

The battle prediction uses a weighted scoring system that considers:

  • Survival Score (40%): HP, defenses, HP regen, and overall defense rating
  • Attack Score (40%): Physical attack, attack speed, offense rating, and skill effect
  • Utility Score (20%): Movement speed, mana regen, and historical win rate
